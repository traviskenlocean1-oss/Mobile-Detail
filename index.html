Got Questions

Frequently Asked
Questions

How long does a detail take? +
Depending on the package and vehicle size, a standard detail takes 1.5–3 hours. A full detail on a larger vehicle may take up to 4 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when you book.
Do you come to my home or office? +
Yes — we come directly to you. Whether you're at home, at work, or anywhere else across South Florida, we bring all the equipment and supplies needed to complete the job on-site.
What areas do you service? +
We serve Pembroke Pines, Miramar, Hollywood, Weston, Cooper City, Davie, Sunrise, Fort Lauderdale, Coral Springs, and surrounding areas in Broward and Miami-Dade County.
What do I need to provide? +
Just your vehicle and access to a power outlet if available (for interior work). We bring all our own professional-grade supplies, equipment, and products — you don't need to provide anything.
How do I book an appointment? +
The easiest way is to call or text Sebastian directly at (937) 303-1014. You can also fill out the contact form on our website. We typically respond within a few hours and get you scheduled fast.
Do you work on all vehicle types? +
Absolutely. We detail sedans, SUVs, trucks, minivans, luxury cars, sports cars, and everything in between. Pricing may vary slightly based on vehicle size — contact us for a free quote.
How much does a detail cost? +
Pricing starts at $60 for a maintenance detail, $80 for interior, $100 for exterior, and $160 for a full detail package. Exact pricing depends on your vehicle size and condition — contact us for a custom quote.
Are your products safe for my vehicle? +
Yes. We use professional-grade, pH-balanced detailing products that are safe for all paint types, leather, fabric, and trim surfaces. We never use harsh chemicals that could damage your vehicle's finish.
